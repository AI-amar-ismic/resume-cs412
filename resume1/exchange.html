What is YES?

Youth Exchange and Study programs (YES) are
full scholarship student exchange programs administered by the U.S. Department of State.
YES includes the "inbound" program for students from close to 40 Muslim dominant countries
to study and live in the U.S., and the "outbound" program, called YES Abroad,
for students from the U.S. to study in selected YES countries.
